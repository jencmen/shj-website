The Firm

Shalev Jencmen & Co. is a leading intellectual property firm in Israel, founded by Adv. and Patent Agent (US) Avi Jencmen and Adv. and Patent Attorney Asaf Shalev. The firm specializes in patents and offers a full scope of legal IP services, with a deep understanding of high-tech, engineering, life sciences, and related fields. Our clients benefit from the personal responsibility of seasoned experts backed by a team of skilled professionals who ensure extraordinary service and reliable results.
